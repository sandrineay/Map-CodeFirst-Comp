Black Techies

Makers Academy - 50 Commercial Street, E1 6LT London

This group is created to help increase collaboration and discussion for Black people in the Computer/Software Technology industry. As one of the smallest demographics in the tech industry, it's easy to feel alone as a black tech professional.
The aim of this group is to bring us together and create a safe space where we can support and inspire one another, while also being a platform for ideas to bring more black people into tech and raise awareness about some of the obstacles black people in tech face.
You do not have to be black to attend this event. We do not want to exclude anyone who supports our cause and wants to learn more about the smallest demographic in tech. We only ask that you respect that we are trying to create a platform for black people first and foremost.
